Requirements:

  • An English copy of Fallout New Vegas with all the DLC from either Steam or GOG
    • (Instructions for properly installing FNV are in the Core module)
  • Windows 10 (64bit) or newer
    • (Windows 7 (64bit) will technically work, but is unsupported)
  • A text editor
    • (I recommend Notepad++, but default Windows notepad will work)
  • An archiving tool (I recommend 7-Zip)
  • Latest GPU drivers are recommended but not necessary (Either NVIDIA or AMD)
  • Atleast 15 gigabytes of free drive space for just the Core module, and 23 gigabytes for the entire guide
  • NOT compatible with DUST / TTW / New California out of the box
    • If you want to use this as a base for either of those mods, you must be aware of the incompatibilities (DUST, TTW and New California)
  • A NexusMods account, register here if you don't have one



Core:

Setup your Fallout New Vegas game
for modding by installing and configuring
Mod Organizer 2 and many other useful tools.
Includes many essential mods/tweaks and bug fixes,
providing the perfect base for anyone looking to mod FNV.

Visuals:

Enhance the visual capabilities of New Vegas

Gameplay:

Overhauls many aspects of the gameplay,
such as the combat and the economy, using many
customizable mods that don't completely change the
core of the game. Centered around difficult yet
rewarding gameplay that stays balanced and true to vanilla.

Content:

Adds many hours of new lore-friendly and balanced
content ranging from new quests to new weapons/armors.
All the new content fits seamlessly with the vanilla game
so you can't tell what is modded and what is vanilla.
