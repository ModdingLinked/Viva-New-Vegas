Visuals: General Information

  • Home

Requirements:

  • A working installation of the Core module
  • 1 gigabyte of free drive space (Low option), 4.5 gigabytes (Medium option), and 7.25 gigabytes (High option)

Screenshots

Module Options:

  • The Visuals module has three options to choose from depending on your system specs; low, medium, and high:
  • Low - No major texture overhauls, just small visual enhancements and new lighting. Very small performance impact if any
  • Medium - Texture overhauls for major objects, such as architecture, landscape, common clutter items, and common conventional firearms. Small to medium performance hit and what I believe to be the best performance/visual fidelity ratio
  • High - Texture overhauls for most common objects in the game. Medium to high performance hit

Recommended Specs:

  • For the Low option, just about any system that can run the Core module can handle it
  • For the Medium option, any semi-modern PC with a quad-core (or dual-core with hyperthreading) that has decent single-core performance, 6+ GB of RAM, and a GPU with 3GB+ of VRAM can handle it
  • For the High option, a modern PC that can run AAA games from the past few years can handle it (New Vegas is notoriously bad at fully utilizing modern hardware, so you don't have to have a super computer by any means)

My System Specs:

  • I personally run the Low option because I like to keep consistency with the style/resolution of textures and I love the consistently perfect performance/stability. However, my system could run the High option just fine
