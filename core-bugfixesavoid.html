Mission Mojave: Breaks more than it fixes, will cause crashes, very outdated, far inferior to YUP

Reload Speed Game Start Fix: Modern, ESP-less implementation in lStewieAl's Tweaks

HeroinZero's Weapon Fixes: Incompatible with WMIM, fixes are more of workarounds than actual fixes

Pretty much any old single bug fix mod: In most cases, these fixes are included in YUP/UPP, and if they are not its most likely because of poor implementation on the mod's end or the bug the mod fixes not actually being considered a bug
